What is the average cost and bandwidth limits per user (in general) in each of the three markets? How may this impact development?

 
  • U.S. 3.9 mbps $2.96
  • Japan 7.9 mbps $2.28
  • Great Britain 3.8 mbps $3.31
 

These results impact development because the application being created has to be able to function on many different bandwidths, and also be a reasonable price. You don't want the application to lag due to speed and you definitely don't want less users due to the cost. Those are two factors to think about when creating an application because those factors can determine how successful an application will be.
